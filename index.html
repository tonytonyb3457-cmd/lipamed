Executive Check-Up Panels

Laboratory Tests

Fasting blood sugar, HbA1c, lipid profile, kidney function (BUN, creatinine, uric acid), liver function (SGPT, SGOT, bilirubin), CBC, Urinalysis.

Cardiovascular Screening

ECG, 2D Echocardiogram (if indicated), stress test (treadmill), and blood pressure monitoring.

Imaging & Cancer Screening

Chest X-ray, ultrasound, mammogram, Pap smear, PSA test, and colonoscopy or fecal occult blood test.
