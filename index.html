Check-Up Panels
Laboratory Tests
Blood chemistry: fasting blood sugar, HbA1c, lipid profile (cholesterol, triglycerides)
Kidney function: BUN, creatinine, uric acid
Liver function: SGPT, SGOT, bilirubin
Complete blood count (CBC)
Urinalysis

Electrocardiogram (ECG)

PSA test (for men over 50)
Colonoscopy or fecal occult blood test (for colorectal cancer screening)
