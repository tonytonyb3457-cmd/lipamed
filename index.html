Comprehensive Assessment

Executive Check-Up Panels

Laboratory Tests

  • Blood chemistry: fasting blood sugar, HbA1c, lipid profile
  • Kidney function: BUN, creatinine, uric acid
  • Liver function: SGPT, SGOT, bilirubin
  • Complete blood count (CBC)
  • Urinalysis

Cardiovascular Screening

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • 2D Echocardiogram (if indicated)
  • Stress test (treadmill exercise test)
  • Blood pressure monitoring

Imaging & Cancer Screening

  • Chest X-ray
  • Ultrasound (abdomen, thyroid, prostate/ovaries)
  • Mammogram (women over 40)
  • Pap smear (women)
  • PSA test (men over 50)
  • Colonoscopy or fecal occult blood test
