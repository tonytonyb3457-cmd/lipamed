Executive Check-Up
Laboratory Tests
Blood chemistry: fasting blood sugar, HbA1c, lipid profile (cholesterol, triglycerides) Kidney function: BUN, creatinine, uric acid Liver function: SGPT, SGOT, bilirubin Complete blood count (CBC) Urinalysis
Cardiovascular Screening
Electrocardiogram (ECG) 2D Echocardiogram (if indicated) Stress test (treadmill exercise test) Blood pressure monitoring
Imaging & Cancer Screening
Chest X-ray Ultrasound (abdomen, thyroid, prostate/ovaries depending on gender) Mammogram (for women over 40) Pap smear (for women) PSA test (for men over 50) Colonoscopy or fecal occult blood test (colorectal cancer screening)
